MARTIN MARKS

This is not me. This is Messi.

My name is Martin Marks. I am from Michigan, but my family originated from Argentina.
I currently attend school at Madonna University and study Computer Science and Business Administration.
I am 21 years old and I love playing soccer and just straight up exploring.
I cant really be still or alone, although I enjoy my freedom, I love discovering the world around me.

I love to play soccer. Coming from Argentina its something my family and I revolved our lives around.
Watching, playing, and observing the game has been something I have done now for about 19 years of my life.
Not only just soccer, I love all sports. Im not good at all of them, but whether its being outside or trying a new sport for the first time, I love being with a band of brothers working together to win.
